BUILDING MOMENTUM
  • By linking people and products via processes we can build gains over time. Students and faculty lead to more projects, lead to more students and faculty. This is how we build institutional memory and momentum.
  • And if you are thinking all these courses, applications, and workbooks are all too much for one person, I say to you: that is surely my point. That is why processes are key. Whatever I think of my own skills, no one person has enough hours in the day to do it all. But, a core of key individuals can act as centers of gravity and propel the orbits of surrounding entities. Thank you very much for you time.
